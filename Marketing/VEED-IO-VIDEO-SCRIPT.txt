VEED.IO SPECIFIC INSTRUCTIONS

Setup:
1. Create new project: 16:9 (YouTube/FB) or 9:16 (IG/TikTok)
2. Import voiceover audio or use Veed.io AI voice (natural, friendly tone)
3. Add royalty-free background music from Veed.io library
4. Use stock footage from Veed.io or Pexels/Unsplash integration

Text Overlay Settings:
- Font: Inter Bold or Montserrat Bold
- Size: Large (easy mobile readability)
- Animation: Fade in + Slide up
- Duration: Sync with voiceover
- Position: Lower third or center (depending on scene)

Transitions:
- Use "Cross Dissolve" between scenes
- Duration: 0.3-0.5 seconds
- Avoid jarring cuts

Export Settings:
- YouTube: 1080p, 60fps, MP4
- Instagram/TikTok: 1080x1920, 30fps, MP4
- Facebook: 1080p or 1080x1080 (square), MP4
- Include subtitles: Auto-generate and review
- Audio: Normalize volume levels

Accessibility:
- Always include subtitles/captions
- High contrast text
- Large, readable fonts
- Avoid flashing effects
